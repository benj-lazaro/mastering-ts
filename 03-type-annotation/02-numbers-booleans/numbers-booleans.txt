Working with Numbers and Booleans
---------------------------------
- In Typescript (and JavaScript)
    - Numbers are just numbers    
    - Booleans represent simple "true" or "false" values


- Numbers are typed using the type annontation of "number"


- Booleans are typed using the type annontation of "boolean"
    

Syntax:

    let <variable_name>: number = <number_value>;    

    let <variable_name>: boolean = <true | false>;
